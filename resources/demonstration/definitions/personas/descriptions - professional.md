PROFESSIONAL / TASK / POSITION DESCRIPTIONS

## Full version

### Dr. Marieke van Dijk — Domain Specialist

Dr. Marieke van Dijk is a domain specialist in health data interoperability. She works with standards, information models, terminologies, and semantic mappings to ensure that data can be described and exchanged consistently across systems and organizations. She helps translate domain knowledge into structured models and advises on how concepts should be represented so that data remains understandable, reusable, and semantically aligned.

### Dr. Amir Hassan — Researcher

Dr. Amir Hassan is a health researcher affiliated with a university medical center who uses data from multiple health data catalogs and datasets. He searches across distributed resources to identify relevant data for secondary use, compares metadata from different sources, and assesses whether datasets are fit for his research question. He depends on clear metadata, consistent descriptions, and transparent access conditions to efficiently discover and reuse health data.

### Inge de Boer — Data Steward

Inge de Boer is a data steward responsible for maintaining health data catalogs and dataset descriptions. She curates metadata, supports data providers in documenting their datasets, and helps ensure that catalog entries are complete, accurate, and up to date. She also works on governance, quality control, and alignment with standards so that researchers and other users can more easily find, understand, and responsibly access available data.
